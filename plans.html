Spectril Green (532nm)

  • 532 nm laser: affordable, reliable, supported.
  • Ideal for early testing and pilot projects.
  • Good for inorganics, polymers, microplastics, halides, cyanides/ammonia.
  • Cost-Effective & Widely Supported
  • Ideal for proof-of-concept projects

Entry-level, cost-effective model

Spectril Red (785nm)

  • 785 nm laser for stronger Raman signals
  • Better for complex organics (pesticides, hydrocarbons, phenols) and biological signatures (algae, bacteria, biofilms)
  • Wider chemical detection range
  • Suited for labs and regulatory use
  • Additional sensors (conductivity, TDS)
  • Greatly-reduced fluorescence

Mid-tier model with enhanced sensitivity

Spectril Pro (785nm)

  • Remote sensing up to 5 meters
  • Integrated real-time microscope
  • Extra sensors: DO, pH, conductivity, TDS
  • Cost-Effective & Widely Supported
  • Additional Sensors (Dissolved O2, pH)
  • Microscope for real-time viewing
  • Scalable storage solutions

Advanced research and field model
